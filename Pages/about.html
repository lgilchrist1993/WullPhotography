All About Wull




William McMahon, Wull Mackers by friends and family. I like to show people and scenes as I see them in my minds eye. I love using manual focus lenses which lends a certain character to an image. Old style shooting with modern equipment. My photography incorporates a lot of street photography and candid shots. I enjoy the candid approach and feel it produces more human and emotive shots.

I specialise in landscapes and candid portraiture, based in South Ayrshire.

Married to my beautiful wife Gillian with 4 Children and 5 grandchildren, the main focus of a lot of my photography. We have a lovely fox red lab named 'Red'. I enjoy cycling, walking around the Scottish landscapes, and listening to old style protest rock and roll. Have been known on occasion to sample some fine wine and real ales.

Prints and commissions are available on request.
